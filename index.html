ABOUT US

With a willing heart to share an Italian Tradition the founders of Goody Pizza poured all their heart and passion to recreate the original wood fired Pizza from Nairobi Kenya. Goody Pizza uses the Mwihoko tomatoes directly from Tharaka Nithi to create their fabulous sauce. Organic and fresh ingredients are the main focus when it comes to create the best Pizza on Earth at Goody Pizza. The taste of these Pizzas will wake up your senses.Your taste buds, will join in a symphony of flavors bringing you to that little Kenyan town with the country side vineyard, the dirt road and that table on the shade under the olive tree.
